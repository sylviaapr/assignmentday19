What is clean eating?

Wondering what clean eating entails? The Clean Eating Diet centers around clean, organic foods and ingredients. It's more than a diet - it's a lifestyle that's based in choosing foods in their natural forms (or as close to it as possible). From clean diet news to clean eating tips to new clean ingredients, you'll find it all here or you can have more detailed information regarding food calories click here.
